Welcome to Taniti!

Explore the beauty, culture, and adventures waiting for you in Taniti. Discover pristine beaches, vibrant nightlife, and rich traditions.


Taniti is a small, tropical island in the Pacific. hile the island has an area of less than 500 square miles, the terrain is varied and includes both sandy and rocky beaches, a small but safe harbor, lush tropical rainforests, and a mountainous interior that includes a small, active volcano. Taniti has an indigenous population of about 20,000. Until a recent increase in tourism, most the Tanitian economy was dominated by fishing or agriculture.

Why Visit Taniti?

Taniti offers a unique blend of relaxation and adventure, perfect for all types of travelers.
