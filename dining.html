Food & Dining

Taniti currently has 10 restaurants: five serve mostly local fish and rice, three serve American-style meals, and two serve Pan-Asian cuisine.

Fine Dining

Taniti hosts one five star dining experience at the Great Reef Resort. You can make reservations by dialing their concierge at 555-123-4567.

Local Delicacies

Grocery Stores

Taniti has two supermarkets, two smaller grocery stores, and one convenience store that is open 24 hours a day.

Featured Experiences

  • Beachside Cafe - Enjoy fresh seafood and stunning ocean views at this local gem.
  • Island Adventures - Join guided tours for snorkeling, kayaking, and exploring hidden beaches.
  • Sunset Spa - Treat yourself to relaxing massages and wellness treatments overlooking the sea.
  • Tiki Rentals - Rent paddleboards, bikes, and beach gear for your adventures.
